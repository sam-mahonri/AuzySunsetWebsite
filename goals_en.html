Motivations
My interest in art has been growing with my age. Every day, month and year that passed my goals had been always taking shape to two main things, reaching semi-realism with realistic painting techniques, and at the same time selling my detailed paintings achieving the most pure, clean and simple trace that is the cartoon style.
Not only my experiences and influences of physique arts were my motivation but also artists inside the internet!
